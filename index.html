Our Story

A Taste of the Highlands

Nestled in the heart of Surat, Highland Restro is a culinary journey through mountain landscapes. We bring together the finest ingredients, time-honoured techniques and a passion for exceptional hospitality.

Every dish on our menu is a tribute to the highlands — bold, honest and crafted with soul. From our wood-fired kitchen to your table, we promise an experience that transcends the ordinary.
